About us

Seeking & achieving justice through innovative application of the law

Global Rights Compliance (GRC) is an international law Foundation dedicated to promoting accountability through the application of international humanitarian law, international criminal law, and human rights law.

Operating in conflict-affected and high-risk regions worldwide, GRC combines legal expertise with practical experience to design responses to atrocity crimes and human rights abuses. Our team are experts at interpreting international law, prosecutorial and investigative analysis, and capacity building, with a focus on delivering mobile support to national and international mechanisms and civil society to promote justice. GRC has been instrumental in supporting high-profile national and international litigation and transitional justice initiatives globally, collaborating with governments, international institutions, and civil society to uphold the rule of law and seek redress for affected communities.
